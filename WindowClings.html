Haunted Window Clings

A Maker Space Project Guide for the Cricut Maker 4

Project Overview

This project guides students through the complete process of creating vibrant window clings. It combines digital design with hands-on crafting, teaching valuable skills in material handling, layering, and digital-to-physical workflows. The result is a unique, customizable Halloween decoration that showcases the student's creativity.
