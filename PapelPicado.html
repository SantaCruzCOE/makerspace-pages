Digital Fabrication

The Digital Crafting Workflow

1

Research & Design

Explore traditional motifs like skulls and flowers. Create a symmetrical, interconnected design in Cricut Design Space.

2

Prepare & Load

Smooth your paper onto a LightGrip mat. Select the correct material setting in the software and load the mat into the Cricut.

3

Cut the Design

Press "Go" and watch the machine perform the subtractive fabrication, precisely cutting away the negative space from your design.

4

Remove & Display

Carefully peel the delicate paper from the mat using a spatula. String your finished pieces together to create a festive banner.

The Power of Precision

The Cricut's digital precision is a game-changer for intricate art forms like Papel Picado. It dramatically increases the success rate for complex designs compared to traditional hand-cutting methods, especially with delicate materials like tissue paper.
